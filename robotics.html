Introduction to Robotics

Robotics and Automation are at the forefront of technological innovation, integrating mechanics, software, and AI to perform complex tasks. From factory lines to surgery rooms, robots are evolving rapidly.

Applications

Industry

Automated assembly lines, quality control systems, and warehouse logistics have been revolutionized.

Medical

Robotic-assisted surgeries, remote diagnostics, and prosthetics are transforming healthcare.

Humanoids

Service robots and social assistants enhance human interaction in public and private spaces.

Future

Autonomous vehicles, drone swarms, and nanobots promise to reshape our world in the coming decades.
